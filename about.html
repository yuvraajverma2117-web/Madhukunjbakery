Our story

Cooked like home, served like family

Madhukunj started as a small, family-run kitchen in Bajna with one simple idea — cook everything the way we'd cook it at home, and treat every guest like family. Over time, the menu grew from chaat and snacks into a full multi-cuisine spread: South Indian, Chinese, thalis, pizzas, and more, all kept strictly pure vegetarian.

Our newest chapter is our homebaked cakes — baked fresh in-house, the first of their kind in town. It's the same philosophy as everything else we make: nothing bought in, nothing sitting around, just made fresh when you order it.
