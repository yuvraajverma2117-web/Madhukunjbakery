Our story

A kitchen that's stayed in the family

Madhukunj began the way most good things in Bajna do — with a family recipe, a small kitchen, and neighbours who kept coming back for more. What started as a few trays of laddoo for festival mornings grew, slowly and honestly, into the shop you see today.

We still cook the way we did at home: fresh batches every day, real ghee and good ingredients, and the same warmth at the counter whether you're picking up a kilo of kaju katli or stopping in for a plate of pav bhaji on your way home.
